Services

Specialist services for temporary works, access, and maintenance.

Our service portfolio covers modular systems, suspended access, rope access, repair and maintenance, and full design-build-maintain solutions.

Temporary Works

Design and construction of access towers, props, shoring, excavation support, falsework, and formwork for safe and efficient execution.

Scaffolding & Formworks

Supported, suspended, mobile, and aerial systems using modular, frame, tube-and-coupler, and rope access methods.

Repair & Maintenance

Scaffold support for maintenance of vertical gardens, chandeliers, and other building interior and exterior works.

Building Envelope & Maintenance

Temporary access and support for cladding, curtain wall, and building envelope maintenance tasks.

Industrial Facilities

Heavy-duty temporary platforms for roll forming machines, plant maintenance, and industrial shutdown works.

Rope Access

Window cleaning, sealant application, and water testing services delivered through rope access technicians.
