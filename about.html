About Us

Making complex temporary works simpler, safer, and more efficient.

S40 Construction provides design-build-maintain temporary works and access solutions across sectors including power generation, industrial facilities, building envelope, building maintenance, transportation, and general construction.

Who We Are

S40 Construction is dedicated to making our clients’ jobs easier through expertise in temporary works. We specialize in the design and construction of scaffolding and formworks, aiming to minimize costs and timeframes while ensuring safety and efficiency.

We engage in the design and construction of temporary works such as scaffolding and formworks and support clients in power generation, industrial facilities, building envelope, building maintenance, transportation, and general construction sectors.

Our Strength

Our team combines technical knowledge, practical field experience, and a strong safety culture to deliver dependable access and support systems for complex project environments. We take a proactive approach to planning, execution, and coordination so that our clients can focus on their core construction goals.

We are known for being responsive, organized, and dependable in the field, especially when projects require precision, fast mobilization, and careful risk management.

What We Deliver

  • Safe and efficient temporary access solutions
  • Custom scaffolding and formwork systems for demanding sites
  • Design-build-maintain services tailored to project requirements
  • Reliable support for maintenance, repair, and shutdown work
  • Flexible solutions for high-risk and time-sensitive construction operations

Industry Focus

Our experience extends across sectors where reliability and safety are essential. We support projects that require strong execution standards, clear communication, and dependable temporary works planning from start to finish.
